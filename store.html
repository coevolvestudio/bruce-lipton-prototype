Store

Our goal is to build the presentation and appeal of the store such that it has a reasonable path to functioning as a small business in its own right.

Knowing that scaling the store into a major ecommerce success is not a priority, our focus here is much more to make sure that it is, and appears to be, a reasonably viable component of the system. Otherwise, the presence of the store actually devalues the rest of the site and gives reason for skepticism about the integrity of the system to the prospective member.
